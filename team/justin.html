About Me

I am Justin William Suseno (薛学刚), Student ID: 1820241085, a sophomore Artificial Intelligence student at the Beijing Institute of Technology. I thrive in team environments and am always seeking opportunities for self-improvement. I enjoy taking on challenges, whether academic or professional, and am committed to making a meaningful impact at BIT and beyond.
